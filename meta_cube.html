你的世界浓缩在一个立方体

在 MetaCube，每个人的世界都浓缩在 3m × 3m × 3m 的空间中。你不再需要广阔的房间，因为每一处功能都在指间滑动之间出现。

In MetaCube, your entire world is condensed into a 3 m × 3 m × 3 m cube. Spacious rooms are obsolete, because every function materialises at your fingertips.

翻转、变形的魔方系统

一块 2m × 2m 的平台通过翻转和组合，可以瞬间变身为 VR 健身区、厨房、浴室、餐桌、录音棚或画室。模块化的设计为你节约每一寸空间。

A reversible 2 m × 2 m platform flips and combines to instantly become a VR gym, kitchen, bathroom, dining table, recording studio or art studio. The modular design saves every centimetre of space for you.

智能环境

通过语音和手势控制，墙壁、地面和家具会响应你的需求，调整温度、光线、音乐，甚至将墙面变为全息屏幕。

Via voice and gesture control, walls, floors and furniture respond to your needs, adjusting temperature, lighting and music, and even turning walls into holographic screens.

无缝切换

模块间无缝衔接，VR 运动结束即刻翻转成为餐桌，烹饪后平台自动清洗并转换为浴室。

Seamless transition between modules: finish a VR workout and flip into a dining table; after cooking, the platform self‑cleans and transforms into a bathroom.

个人定制

根据你的职业与爱好，调整 Cube 内部的功能模块与风格，让每个空间都是为你定制的工作室。

Tailor the interior to your profession and passions, adjusting functional modules and styles so that every space becomes a studio designed for you.

可持续生活

高效能源管理、循环水系统以及可回收材料，让你的生活在舒适中保持低碳。

Efficient energy management, water recycling and recyclable materials ensure a comfortable yet low‑carbon lifestyle.

魔方大厦：城市的未来

整个建筑是一座巨大的“魔方大厦”，由数以千计的 Cube 组成。系统根据住户需求动态调配 Cube，让社区随时变化，灵动如生命。

The entire structure is a massive “Rubik’s Cube” composed of thousands of cubes. The system dynamically allocates cubes according to residents’ needs, making the community fluid and alive.
